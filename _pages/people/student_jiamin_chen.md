Jiamin Chen received her B.S. degree in mechanical engineering from Shanghai Jiao Tong University in 2022, and her M.S. degree in industrial and systems engineering from University of Florida in 2024. She is now pursuing a Ph.D. degree in industrial and systems engineering at Texas A&M University. Her research interests include stochastic programming and integer programming.
